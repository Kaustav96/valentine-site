# How to Add Your Photos and Videos

Add your special memories here to show them in the calendar modals!

## Required Files:

### July 2025:
- `july10-1.jpg` - Photo from when you first met (July 10, 2025)

### August 2025:
- `aug20-1.jpg` - Photo of the letter
- `aug20-2.jpg` - Photo of the food (chicken & paratha)

### January 2026:
- `jan10-1.jpg` - Photo from the scooty ride
- `jan10-video.mp4` (optional) - Video from the scooty ride

## Instructions:
1. Copy your photos/videos to this folder
2. Rename them to match the names above, OR
3. Update the file names in `src/components/Calendar.js` to match your files

## Supported formats:
- Images: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .gif
- Videos: .mp4, .mov, .webm

The app will show placeholders if files are not found, so you can add them gradually!
